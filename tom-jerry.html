Tom & Jerry

Tom and Jerry, American animated cartoon series about a hapless cat’s never-ending pursuit of a clever mouse. Tom and Jerry is an iconic American animated media franchise centered on the endless, slapstick rivalry between a hapless house cat named Tom and a clever mouse named Jerry. Created in 1940 by animators William Hanna and Joseph Barbera for Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer (MGM), the series is celebrated for its visual humor and classic chases
